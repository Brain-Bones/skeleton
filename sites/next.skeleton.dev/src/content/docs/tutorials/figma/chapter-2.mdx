## Import Custom Theme


<Process>
    <ProcessStep step="1">
		### Open Skeleton v3 Figma Library

		<b>Disclaimer:</b> The plugin will work only with Skeleton v3 Figma Library.
			
		<Image src={ImgOpenSkeleton} class="rounded-container" alt="Create New Project" />
    </ProcessStep>
    <ProcessStep step="2">
		### Open Skeleton Themes plugin

		Click on the ‘Actions’ button on the toolbar at the bottom, search for ‘Skeleton Themes’, and click on ‘Skeleton Themes’ to run the plugin.

		<Image src={ImgOpenThemePlugin} class="rounded-container" alt="Create New Project" />
    </ProcessStep>
    <ProcessStep step="3">
		### Add new theme

		The library comes preconfigured with the Theme `Cerberus`. To add your theme, click on ‘Add theme’.  

		<Image src={ImgAddNewTheme} class="rounded-container" alt="Import Project" />
    </ProcessStep>
    <ProcessStep step="4">
		### Select theme

		Open the <a href="https://themes.skeleton.dev/themes/create"> Skeleton Theme Generator,</a> click `Import` at the top, and select your favorite theme. Alternatively, you can compose your own.

		<Image src={ImgSelectTheme} class="rounded-container" alt="Publish" />
    </ProcessStep>
</Process>

<Process>
    <ProcessStep step="5">
		### Copy theme

Click the `Code` tab on the right panel and copy the theme.

		<Image src={ImgCopyTheme} class="rounded-container" alt="Create New File" />
    </ProcessStep>
    <ProcessStep step="6">
		### Paste theme

		Go back to the `Skeleton Themes` plugin in Figma, paste the theme code into it, and click ‘Upload.’

        <b>Note:</b> Depending on your machine capacity, theme uploading may take 3-5 mins.
		<Image src={ImgPasteTheme} class="rounded-container" alt="Select Library" />

    </ProcessStep>
    <ProcessStep step="7">
		### Publish updates

		After the theme is uploaded, we need to publish the library to make the new theme available in your project files.
On the left panel, click on the `Book` icon. Then, click on the blue `Publish...` button.

		<Image src={ImgPublishUpdates} class="rounded-container" alt="Use Library" />
        After that, another modal will appear. Click the `Publish` button there again. 
<b>Note:</b> Publishing a new theme may take 5-7 minutes.
<Image src={ImgPublish} class="rounded-container" alt="Use Library" />
    </ProcessStep>
</Process>

## Import Custom Theme

<ul class="list-inside list-disc space-y-2">
Precondition:
	<li>Completed <a href="#">Chapter 1: Set-up Skeleton Figma library</a></li>
	<li>Completed <a href="#">Chapter 2: Import Custom Theme</a></li>
	
</ul>

<Process>
    <ProcessStep step="1">
		### Open project

		Open the project where you use the `Skeleton Figma Library`. 
		<Image src={ImgOpenProject} class="rounded-container" alt="Use Library" />	
		
    </ProcessStep>

     <ProcessStep step="2">
		### Set the stage

		Add ‘page examples’ components on the canvas to see how it works.
		<Image src={ImgSetStage} class="rounded-container" alt="Use Library" />	
		
    </ProcessStep>

     <ProcessStep step="3">
		### Switch theme

		On the right panel, in the ‘Pages’ section, click `Apply variable mode`, hover over the `Theme` option, and select your theme (Chapter 2: Import Custom Theme).
		<Image src={ImgSwitchTheme} class="rounded-container" alt="Use Library" />
        Voila! You should see your theme applied to your components 	
		
        <Image src={ImgSwitchColorSchemeResult} class="rounded-container" alt="Use Library" />
    </ProcessStep>

     <ProcessStep step="4">
		### Switch modes

		Each `theme` in Skeleton has a `light` and `dark` mode by default. You can switch `modes` in the same way as `themes`.

On the right panel, click `Apply variable mode` in the `Pages` section, hover over the `Color Scheme` option, and select your theme. 
		 <Image src={ImgSwitchColorScheme} class="rounded-container" alt="Use Library" />
         Your components are now in `Dark` `Color Scheme`
    <Image src={ImgSwitchColorSchemeResult} class="rounded-container" alt="Use Library" />
		
    </ProcessStep>

</Process>
