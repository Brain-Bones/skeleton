## Using PNPM

https://pnpm.io/

Skeleton makes use of [PNPM workspaces](https://pnpm.io/workspaces) to create and maintain the Skeleton monorepo. As such you will be required to install and use PNPM to contribute to Skeleton. Don't worry though, it's free and easy to pickup and learn if you're familiar with Node or Yarn.
